# Baromedr Cymru Wave 2 — High-Priority Policy Questions

These questions are designed for WCVA policy teams to interrogate the data further. Each is grounded in patterns identified in the Wave 2 dataset (n = 111).

---

## Volunteer Recruitment

1. **Why is word-of-mouth still the dominant recruitment channel despite digital tools being widely used?**
   - 80/111 orgs use word of mouth; 74 use social media; 71 use own digital channels. Yet "low response to recruitment activities" is the #1 barrier (42 orgs). Are digital methods reaching the right audiences, or is there a digital-to-action conversion gap?

2. **Are small organisations disproportionately affected by recruitment barriers?**
   - Small orgs may lack dedicated volunteer managers, budgets for outreach, or capacity to onboard. Cross-segmenting barriers by org size could reveal whether tailored support (e.g. shared recruitment infrastructure via CVCs) would help.

3. **What would it take to move organisations from "trying everything" to "trying the right things"?**
   - Organisations report using 4–5 methods on average but still experiencing difficulty. Is there evidence that certain method combinations are more effective for specific activity types or geographies?

## Volunteer Retention

4. **What's the relationship between what organisations offer volunteers and their retention success?**
   - Expenses coverage is the most common offering (74 orgs), but only 19 offer financial advice signposting. Do organisations that offer a broader package report lower retention difficulty?

5. **Can anything mitigate the external retention factors (work changes, caring responsibilities)?**
   - The top barriers are largely outside organisational control. Does offering flexible or micro-volunteering options reduce the impact? Wave 3 could test this with a targeted question.

## Demand and Financial Sustainability

6. **What's the gap between demand growth and capacity to meet it?**
   - 62% report rising demand, 35% report worsening finances. How many organisations are in "both" categories simultaneously? What's their operating likelihood compared to the rest?

7. **How sustainable is reserves usage at current rates?**
   - Median reserves cover 6 months. 28% have made unplanned use. At what point does this become a sector-wide solvency risk? Can we model a "reserves runway" scenario?

## Geographic and Structural Patterns

8. **How do geographic patterns in volunteer shortages map to deprivation indices?**
   - Cardiff dominates the sample (23%). Do areas with higher deprivation report worse recruitment/retention? This would require linking to WIMD (Welsh Index of Multiple Deprivation) data.

9. **Are there meaningful differences between organisations that serve locally vs. nationally?**
   - Cross-segmenting by `wales_scope` could reveal whether locally-focused orgs face different pressures than those operating across Wales or nationally.

## Earned Settlement

10. **Is the earned settlement policy an opportunity or an unfunded mandate for the sector?**
    - 42 orgs agree/strongly agree with the principle, but most would need additional resources/guidance. What specific support would tip the balance? Can WCVA proactively design an implementation toolkit?

## Methodological / Data Quality

11. **How representative is the Wave 2 sample?**
    - 111 respondents from a sector of ~30,000+ organisations. Which activity types, sizes, or regions are under-represented? Can weighting or targeted Wave 3 recruitment improve coverage?

12. **What trend signals emerge when comparing with Wave 1?**
    - Are the recruitment/retention difficulties new or persistent? Is the financial deterioration accelerating? Even directional comparisons (better/worse/same) would add significant analytical value.

---

## How to Use These Questions

- **Policy briefings**: Select 3–5 questions most relevant to current policy priorities
- **Wave 3 design**: Use gaps identified here to add targeted questions
- **Stakeholder conversations**: Frame questions around "what would it take to change this?"
- **Cross-data linkage**: Several questions benefit from external datasets (WIMD, ONS, TSSW network data)
